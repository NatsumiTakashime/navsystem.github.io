STUDENT ACTIVITY CENTER

It is the building where students perform different programs coordinated by the organizers, facilitator, and etc. The activities done cover both extracurricular activities through the Assumption Student Council and Student Organizations as well as co-curricular activities which are coordinated with Department Chairpersons. Activities are geared towards the total development of students and to their education in community living and leadership.
